About Colora Podcast

Colora Podcast is a completely independent and award-winning podcast production company. We aim at helping listeners be aware of the things they don’t think about, better understand each other and the whole world. Colora Podcast was founded in 2010 and is located in the heart of Brooklyn, New York. Our podcasts are downloaded more than ten million times per month by listeners from nearly 190 countries across the world. All of our shows are powered by you, our listener.
